How to Play Guessify

  1. Guess the hidden 5-letter word in 6 tries or less.
  2. Type a 5-letter word and press Enter to submit your guess.
  3. After each guess, the color of the tiles will change:
    • means the letter is correct and in the right position.
    • means the letter is in the word but in the wrong position.
    • means the letter is not in the word.
  4. Use Delete or the "Del" button to remove letters.
  5. All guesses must be valid 5-letter words in our dictionary.
  6. Letters can appear more than once in a word.

Good luck!
